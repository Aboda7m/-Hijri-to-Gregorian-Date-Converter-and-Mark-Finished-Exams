Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024-05-31

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any user data at all. All data accessed are the Hijri dates, which are used locally to calculate the Gregorian date. This data is not collected by us and never reaches our servers.

Local Data Usage

The Hijri dates are used solely on your local device to perform the necessary calculations to convert to the Gregorian date. No data is transmitted or stored outside your device.
